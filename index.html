Programs Offered

Junior High School

Junior High School is a lower secondary education which comprises four grade levels – Grades 7 to 10. Learners who completed elementary education in any DepEd accredited private and public elementary schools are eligible for this program. The learning areas include Filipino, English, Mathematics, Science, Araling Panlipunan, Edukasyon sa Pagpapakatao (EsP), MAPEH, and TLE, providing competencies and 21st-century skills. A Certificate of Completion is given after successful completion of the curriculum.

Senior High School

Senior High School is two years of specialized upper secondary education. Students may choose from the Academic Track (General Academic Strand) or the Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track. The TVL track is designed for students who wish to work after graduation by giving them vocational skills such as Bread and Pastry Production, Food and Beverage, Electrical Installation and Maintenance, and Refrigeration and Air Conditioning.

Academic Track

General Academic Strand (GAS) is designed for students who are undecided on what course to take in college. It includes subjects such as Applied Economics, Organization and Management, Humanities, Social Sciences, and Disaster Preparedness, preparing learners for various career paths.

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood Track (TVL)

The TVL track equips students with the skills needed for work right after senior high school. It offers courses like Bread and Pastry Production, Food and Beverage, Electrical Installation and Maintenance, and Refrigeration and Air Conditioning.

Alternative Learning System (ALS)

The Alternative Learning System (ALS) is a parallel learning system providing opportunities for out-of-school youth and adult learners to develop literacy and skills. Dr. Arcadio Santos NHS offers ALS courses like Literacy, Accreditation and Equivalency (ACE), InfEd, Basic Computer, Electrical Installation and Maintenance, Refrigeration and Air Conditioning, and Bread and Pastry Production.
